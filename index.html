Welcome to Campricorn!

Planning a camping trip and want to make sure you can stargaze? Enter a state code or a campground name along with the date of your trip. Once you select your campground, we'll generate a star chart for that area and date, as well as some info about the campground itself. We hope this helps you decide your next campsite and visit us again soon for your next trip!
